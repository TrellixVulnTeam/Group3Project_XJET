About Us

Ethni-connect is a user friendly, quick search platform to get home-cooked food and interpreter services based on Ethnicity. We operate based on diversified ethnic groups. This is a platform which benefits both customers and vendors. It helps home chefs to showcase their unique culinary skills to the world with regular income and benefits customers who are craving for homemade delicacies. We are not just limited with food service. We are planning to put our new interpreter services based on your preferred ethnicity soon!!! Our goal is to help to improve worldwide communication through accurate, interpretation services, and a wide variety of tailored language solutions for individuals, organizations, and businesses of all sizes. No matter how intricate your request, we make it happen!
